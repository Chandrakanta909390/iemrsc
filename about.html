About IEMRSC

The Research Scholar Conclave (IEMRSC) is a national-level platform designed to bring together brilliant minds from across India. It encourages innovation, research excellence, and impactful ideas through presentations and discussions.


Participants will showcase their research, exchange knowledge, and connect with scholars, mentors, and industry experts. The event aims to inspire creativity and foster a culture of learning and collaboration.
